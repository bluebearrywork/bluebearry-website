Core Capabilities

Every Detail Built for Desktop Live2D

From anchor tracking to stream-ready visibility, Live2DCursor packs a VTuber-grade desktop interaction experience into one lightweight app.

Anchor Tracking System

Auto-detect, click to select, or search by ArtMesh ID to precisely lock the model's “gaze point” so its eyes always follow your cursor.

Drag Physics

Dragging the model triggers springy physics feedback with an adjustable gain, so every tug feels soft and full of weight.

Expressions & Hotkeys

Trigger expressions from any key or mouse button, with Hold / Toggle modes and fully configurable fade in/out timing.

OBS-Friendly Background Mode

Transparent background + always-on-top + off-screen rendering: the window hides from view but keeps rendering, perfect for stream capture.

Global Hotkeys & Click-Through

One key toggles a click-through, always-on-top overlay, plus overlay/fullscreen mode and model switching — every hotkey is customizable.

Multi-Model Management

Drag a .model3.json onto the window to load it — switch between characters anytime, each with its own scale and position offset.

Core Features

Every Feature Built for Live2D + Mouse & Keyboard Interaction

Easily enjoy the fun of interacting with your Live2D model!

Anchor Tracking System

Auto-detect, click to select, or search by ArtMesh ID to precisely lock the model's “anchor point,” so it always follows your cursor.

Drag Physics

Dragging the model triggers its physics feedback, with adjustable gain.

Expressions & Hotkeys

Trigger expressions from any key or mouse button, with Hold / Click modes and fully configurable fade in/out timing.

OBS-Friendly Background Mode

Transparent background + always-on-top + off-screen rendering: the window hides from view but keeps rendering, perfect for stream capture.

Global Hotkeys & Click-Through

One key toggles a click-through, always-on-top overlay, plus overlay/fullscreen mode and model switching — every hotkey is customizable.

Multi-Model Management

Drag a .model3.json onto the window to load it — switch between characters anytime, with settings you can save individually to your computer.

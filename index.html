Live2DCursor locks an anchor point on your Live2D model straight onto your mouse cursor — wherever your cursor goes, your model goes with it. Core Capabilities

Every Detail Built to Bind Live2D to Your Cursor

From cursor anchor tracking to stream-ready visibility, Live2DCursor packs a VTuber-grade cursor-bound interaction experience into one lightweight app.

Anchor Tracking System

Auto-detect, click to select, or search by ArtMesh ID to pick any point on your model — lock it to your mouse cursor and the whole model rides along wherever you move.

Drag Physics

Because the model is anchored to your cursor, swinging it around whips up springy physics feedback, with an adjustable gain so every movement feels soft and full of weight.

Expressions & Hotkeys

Trigger expressions from any key or mouse button, with Hold / Toggle modes and fully configurable fade in/out timing.

OBS-Friendly Background Mode

Transparent background + always-on-top + off-screen rendering: the window hides from view but keeps rendering, perfect for stream capture.

Global Hotkeys & Click-Through

One key toggles a click-through, always-on-top overlay, plus overlay/fullscreen mode and model switching — every hotkey is customizable.

Multi-Model Management

Drag a .model3.json onto the window to load it — switch between characters anytime, each with its own scale and position offset.
